Cyber Defense Teaching Cyber Security to K6-K12 Students through Immersive Gaming

What is Cyber Defense?

Cyber Defense is a cyber security game that is built for kids between 6th and 12th grade. Our goal is to help younger generations learn and get involved in cyber security or a STEM related discipline from an early age. Our application is designed to integrate multiple areas of STEM education into cyber security games, thus increasing student education and introduction to a diverse set of topics.

Anticipated Learning Outcomes

  • Expose student to the threats that exist in modern computing environments and networks
  • Teach STEM principles through interactive cyber security games
  • Demonstrate the importance of STEM, Cyber Security and Computer Science through interactive and immersive games
  • Student is excited about using the application
  • Student wants to tell their friends and family what they learned

Instructions - How To Play

Cyber Defense is made of 3 difficulties. Under each difficulty there are 3 levels. Each level is modeled after a real life cyber security issue and your goal and task is to understand the security vulnerabilty and fix it before the attackers. In order to advance in the game, you must successfully complete a level in order to unlock and advance to the next one. You will be a member of the Blue Team (Defense Team) playing against the Red Team (The Offense Team - Pen Testers and Blackhats). To stop the Red team we must stop all of the attacks that Red team tries to use against us. At the end of every mission, your stats can be found on the rewards menu. Your statistics tell you how well you did against the Red team's attacks. After denying the Red teams attack several times, the difficulty of the Red teams attacks will become increasingly harder. As a Blue team member, we look for the strongest people to help defend against the severity of the Red teams attacks. We only accept the best candidates to help protect our team. Are you up for the challenge?

How does Cyber Defense work?

Cyber Defense is a new and innovative cyber security educational gaming platform. We have developed a mini puzzle that works to test students knowledge while providing a fun and immersive cyber security themed game. These games have the student solve "attacks" that are relaying STEM and cyber security based material into the learning platform of a video game that captivates and sustains the student's attention. Cyber Defense opens up the minds of students by encouraging them to think bigger and by correlating real life cyber security examples into the story based game curriculum. Through the integration of real life situations in the mini games we hope to encourage students to strive to continue doing well in school and pursue a career in STEM related disciplines. Cyber security is growing at an exceptionally rapid pace, we hope to provide and continue development on CyberDefense, our innovative, intuitive, and easy to use cyber security gaming platform, that students across the world will love to use.

About Our Team

We are a group computer science students at University of West Floria. Our group has partaken in what is called a hack-a-thon or some may call it a coding fest. At this years event our topic was a way to help get STEM learning injected into middle and high school classrooms.

Our group decided the best way to help get STEM learning into the classrooms is by creating a web page game that would help teach students about cyber security and in doing so incorporate STEM into the lives of the students without having the students realize it at first glance.
